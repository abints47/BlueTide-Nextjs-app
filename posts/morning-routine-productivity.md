## Rethinking the Morning
Most productivity advice about mornings is either too extreme (cold plunges at 5 AM) or too vague ("just wake up earlier"). Here are five habits that are actually sustainable.

### 1. Delay Your Phone by 30 Minutes
Checking notifications first thing puts you in reactive mode before you've even decided what your day should look like.

### 2. Hydrate Before Caffeine
A glass of water before coffee helps kickstart your metabolism and counteracts the mild dehydration from sleep.

### 3. Write Three Priorities
Not a full to-do list — just three things that would make today feel successful if you finished them.

### 4. Move Your Body, Even Briefly
A 10-minute walk or stretch session is enough to shift your mental state and reduce stiffness.

### 5. Protect the First Hour
Whatever you do in your first waking hour tends to set the tone for the rest of the day — guard it accordingly.

## The Takeaway
None of these habits require waking up at dawn or overhauling your life. Small, consistent choices compound into a noticeably different quality of day.
