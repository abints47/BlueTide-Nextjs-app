## Why Less Is More
Every seasoned traveler eventually learns the same lesson: the less you carry, the more freedom you have. No checked baggage fees, no lost luggage anxiety, no lugging a heavy suitcase up cobblestone streets.

### The Core Wardrobe Method
Pick a color palette of 3-4 shades that all mix and match. This lets you build dozens of outfit combinations from just 10-12 clothing items.

### Multi-Purpose Gear
Choose items that serve more than one function:
*   A sarong that works as a towel, blanket, or beach cover-up
*   A packable jacket that compresses to the size of a water bottle
*   Merino wool clothing that resists odor and can be worn multiple days

### Digital Minimalism Too
Don't forget your digital load. Download offline maps, e-books, and playlists before you leave so you're not dependent on constant connectivity.

## Final Thoughts
Packing light isn't about deprivation — it's about intention. Every item earns its place in your bag, and that same clarity often follows you into how you experience the trip itself.
